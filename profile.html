About Me

Design Meets Engineering

I am Apurbo Podder, an Industrial and Product Designer passionate about transforming ideas into refined, production-ready products.

My primary expertise is luxury eyewear design, where every surface, proportion and detail is carefully engineered to balance aesthetics, comfort and manufacturability.

Beyond eyewear, I work on CAD development, product visualization, concept generation, rapid prototyping and realistic rendering, helping products move efficiently from concept to production.

Philosophy

Every Product Should Tell
A Story Before It Is Manufactured.

My design process begins by understanding the user's needs, manufacturing constraints and business goals.

Through thoughtful engineering, precision CAD modeling and realistic visualization, I aim to create products that feel timeless, purposeful and technically sound.

I believe exceptional products emerge when functionality, engineering and aesthetics work together seamlessly.
